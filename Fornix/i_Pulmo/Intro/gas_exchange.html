  • Referential range

    • PaO2: 90 ± 10 mmHg
      ≤ 60: respiratory failure
    • PaCo2: 40 ± 5 mmHg
    • pH: 7.40 ± 0.05
    • HCO3-: 24 ± 2 mEq/L

  • CO2

  • Respiratory faillure

  • AG

  • A-aDO2 (Alveolar-artery)

    • A-aDO2 = 150 - PaCO2/0.8 - PaO2
      Ref: ≤ 15 Torr

    A-aDO2
    = PAO2 - PaO2
    = PIO2 - PaCO2/R - PaO2
    = 150 - PaCO2/0.8 - PaO2

    PIO2: inspiratory

    R: ratio
