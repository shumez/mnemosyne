ADEM Acute Disseminated Encephalomyelitis aka, postinfectious encephalomyelitis

Keyword

Keyword

Definition

Demyelinating disease of the central nervous system that typically presents as a monophasic disorder associated with multifocal neurologic symptoms and encephalopathy
